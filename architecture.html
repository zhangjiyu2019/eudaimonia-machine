Core Architecture

The Happiness Machine is a three-tiered control system designed to translate the subjective “problem of suffering” into an objective engineering solution. By aligning artificial intelligence with the biological first principles of human flourishing, we transition from a species of unconscious struggle to one of conscious self-design.

I · Psychological state recognition

The Psyche–AI alignment

Maps subjective state into a high-dimensional signature (arousal, valence, dominance, time-horizon); infers it from biometrics, neural signals, and behavior; steers toward meaningful striatal reward, vmPFC–ACC congruence, insular integration, and a quieter DMN.
