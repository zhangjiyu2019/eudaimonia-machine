The Methodology

The Bio-Digital Pivot

To solve a subjective problem with objective tools, we translate "qualities" of experience into "quantities" of data. We triangulate subjective experience using three streams:

  • Biological Signal (Hardware): Heart Rate Variability (HRV), Galvanic Skin Response, Cortisol.
  • Neurological Signal (OS): EEG/fMRI data to detect overactivity in the Default Mode Network.
  • Behavioral Signal (Output): Voice prosody, typing cadence, and micro-expressions.
