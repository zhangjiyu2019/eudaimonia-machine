The Transition Timeline

Phase 1: The Incubation (Years 0–5)

The Scope: Early Adopters and Systems Thinkers.

The "Eudaimonia Machine" initially exists as a personal prototype. A small group of pioneers begin to actively collaborate with AI, taking suggestions from AI to map their bio-feedback. They integrate these suggestions into their schedules, resulting in a radical drop in cortisol and a massive spike in flow state capacity.

Phase 2: The Expansion & The Filter (Years 5–15)

The Scope: Institutional Adoption.

Health systems and corporations realize that Eudaimonic alignment vastly improves human capability. The AI filters the noise of global tragedy not to deceive the user, but to protect their functional capacity. In this ideal future, long term speaking, nobody would be able to see wars in news. The aggressive impulses of the population are regulated by individual bio-feedback loops.
